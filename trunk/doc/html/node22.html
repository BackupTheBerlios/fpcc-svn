Enforcement settings

In this part we explain the settings of the QoS enforcement part at the PEP, what is also done within an XML file. This file has two parts, one for general settings, and one for predefined rules, that can be activated at startup of the PEP, or later through the PDP.

See files pep-rule-core.xml and pep-rule-user.xml for details.
